Project Title :

AireLine Report and Dashboard

Project Scope Statement:

In this project I want to show following things on dashboard about Aireline route

1. What are the Total Number of flight in a day

2.Find the busiest day at particular Airport

3. What are the total number of flight per day in a month

4. What are the top 10 busiest flight routes

5. Which are the top 10 longest flight routes

6. Represent the flight flying outside particular (in this dashboard i take SFO airtport to visualise airport and dashboard) towards different parts of the world.
